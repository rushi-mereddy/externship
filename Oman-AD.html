Frequently Asked Questions

Find Answers to the Most Popular Questions

1. What is an Externship?

Externships are experiential learning opportunities, similar to internships but generally shorter, provided by Organizations to give students a short practical experiences in their field of study.

2. Do I need to have prior knowledge to work on the project?

You may require some basic knowledge in the respective technology that you wanted to enroll for. However we would provide necessary Learning Resources for the project so that you can understand the concepts before actually developing the whole project.

3. Can I participate as a Team?

No. This program is for individual eligible students only, team participation is not acceptable.

4. Will there be any guidance provided to complete the Project?

Yes, Dedicated Mentor support is provided to complete your project

5. Do I get Certificate?

Yes. You will get an Externship Certificate from SmartInternz & Project Completion Certificate from Partnered Company on Project Approval by the Mentor

6. How will I connect with Mentors?

Yes. You will be connected to respective mentors through communication channels provided by us.
